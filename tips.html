Health Tips

💊 If You Miss a Dose

If you forget to take your medicine:

  1. Check the medication instructions
  2. If it's almost time for your next dose, skip the missed one
  3. Never take double doses unless instructed
  4. Ask your pharmacist if unsure

🥤 Taking with Water

Always take pills with a full glass of water unless otherwise directed.

⏰ Consistent Timing

Try to take your medicine at the same time each day for best results.

🌡️ Proper Storage

Most medicines should be stored in a cool, dry place away from sunlight.
